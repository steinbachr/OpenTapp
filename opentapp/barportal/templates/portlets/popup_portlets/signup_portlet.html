What is drinkUp?

drinkUp is a promotional and social media platform that focuses exclusively on drink specials. You choose the specials that your bar offers, and patrons use the mobile app on their smartphones to access these specials as well as other bar information. Specials can be for anyone or only for groups of a specified size. Patrons connect with their friends through the mobile application and plan to linkUp and drinkUp at your bar!
